Why The Personalized Knee may be the right fit for your knee

A knee as unique as you are

The Persona® Knee implant offers multiple size and shape options to best fit your unique knee.

Faster recovery

The ROSA Robotic System has helped patients recover faster compared to non-robotic surgery.

Lower pain scores

Thanks to ROSA—less “ow” and more “wow” has been demonstrated compared to non-robotic surgery.

Improved range of motion

Patients achieve a better range of motion with ROSA than with traditional surgery—because bending your new knee shouldn’t feel like a rusty lawn chair.

Not all patients are candidates. Results are not necessarily typical, indicative or representative of all recipient patients as recovery times will vary and some patients continue to experience pain or poor range of motion. Talk to your surgeon about whether knee replacement is right for you and other risks, including but not limited to implant wear, infection, loosening, bone fracture, and implant breakage or failure, any of which could require additional surgery.
